Builder Use Cases

Smart Agents are AI models capable of utlizing Web3 wallets. Smart Agents are capable of detecting the intent or users or other agents and contructing wallet-based transactions for the user of agent to sign. By extending AI agent capabilities on-chain, Smart Agents open up a new paradigm between AI agents and blockchain protocols.

The accelerator will be backing use cases that drive adoption of models and compute through user-facing frontends, agent-protocol interfaces, or agent-to-agent interfaces.
