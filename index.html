Accelerate Builders

The Morpheus Builder Accelerator is designed to put Builders first and help pave the way for open source software to utilize Morpheus and the Techno Capital Machine (TCM). The initial focus is on the development of Smart Agents using Morpheus and helping them access Code, Capital, Compute and Community.

  • Code. Assist builders as they navigate the Morpheus code base and provide engineering resources during early development.
  • Capital. Advise builders on paths to raise capital, including the MOR20 fair launch standard, and introduce them to capital providers.
  • Compute. Provide exclusive compute deals to support bootstrapping your product.
  • Community. Provide access to mentorship from the community based on the specific needs of projects.

Smart Agent Use Cases

Smart Agents are AI models capable of utlizing Web3 wallets. Smart Agents are capable of detecting the intent or users or other agents and contructing wallet-based transactions for the user of agent to sign.

By extending AI agent capabilities on-chain, Smart Agents open up a new paradigm between AI agents and blockchain protocols. While the use cases of Smart Agents are limitless, below is a curated a list to enable the first generation of Morpheus hackers.
