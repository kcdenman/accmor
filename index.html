Morpheus Builder Accelerator

Apply Now

The Morpheus Builder Accelerator is designed to put Builders first and help pave the way for open source software to utilize Morpheus and the Techno Capital Machine (TCM). The initial focus is on the development of Smart Agents using Morpheus and helping them access Code, Capital, Compute and Community.

Code

Smart Agent development tooling is available at moragents.

Capital

The MOR20 Fair Launch standard offers your project a novel way raise capital via a fair token launch.

Compute

Access low-cost and decentralized compute resources using the Morpheus-Lumerin-Node implementation.

Builder Acceleration Benefits

As an accelerated Builder, your project will have access to valuable benefits from the Morpheus community as the community is naturally incentivized to support builders.

Mentorship

You will receive direct support from mentors with deep experience launching protocols, building agents, and running infrastructure. They will be available to help your project navigate the Morpheus and Smart Agent ecosystem.

Builder Rewards

The Morpheus protocol emissions stream 24% of tokens to builders with the most community support. Your project will be able to maximize Builder Rewards through its participation in the accelerator.

Compute Budget

A compute budget will be made available to your project enabling you to access models and compute needed to develop and launch your project.

Token Launch Support

If you choose to launch a token using the MOR20 smart contract standard, you will have access to code, capital, and monetors with MOR20 Fair Launch experience. Your MOR20 Fair Launch will also benefit from more community mindshare.

Smart Agent Use Cases

Smart Agents are AI models capable of utlizing Web3 wallets. Smart Agents are capable of detecting the intent or users or other agents and contructing wallet-based transactions for the user of agent to sign. By extending AI agent capabilities on-chain, Smart Agents open up a new paradigm between AI agents and blockchain protocols.

The accelerator will be backing use cases that drive adoption of models and compute through user-facing frontends, agent-protocol interfaces, or agent-to-agent interfaces.
